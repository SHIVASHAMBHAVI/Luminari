History & Insights

The Great Pyramid of Giza is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. Built during the Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom of Egypt, it was constructed as a tomb for the Pharaoh Khufu. It is the largest of the three pyramids in the Giza pyramid complex and was the tallest man-made structure in the world for over 3,800 years.
